## Features

- **Green & White Theme** with black text for readability.  
- **Hero Slider** powered by Swiper.js with 3 images.  
- **HubSpot Form** embedded for manufacturers to submit details.  
- **Benefits Section** with alternating image and text layout.  
- **How It Works Section**: step-by-step guide to selling globally.  
- **CTA Buttons** to become a verified supplier.  
- **Responsive Design**: works on mobile, tablet, and desktop.  
- **Premium Look** with proper padding, rounded images, and clean typography.

## Notes

- Replace `logo.png` with your official Raw Aura logo.
